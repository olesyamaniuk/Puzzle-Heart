About The Game

Puzzle Heart Match-3 Adventure takes players on an emotional journey through a world filled with challenging puzzles and captivating stories. Solve match-3 puzzles to uncover secrets, restore a magical realm, and reconnect broken hearts. With stunning visuals, immersive storylines, and innovative gameplay mechanics, this game promises hours of fun and intrigue for puzzle enthusiasts.

How to Play

  • Match and Solve

    Match three or more heart-shaped tiles to clear the board and progress through levels.

  • Complete Objectives

    Fulfill level-specific goals, such as collecting items, breaking barriers, or unlocking new areas.

  • Utilize Boosters
